Building

Bagley Vision reintroduces a century-old building with a renewed sense of purpose. Historic character has been carefully preserved and reimagined through a modern lens, where original craftsmanship meets contemporary design. The result is a space that honors its past while confidently stepping into its next chapter.

Preserving History Image

Preserving History

Bagley Vision has preserved the building's authentic character. Tall, exposed ceilings and large timber beams throughout showcase the building's history. These historic elements create a distinctive backdrop for contemporary living and commerce, providing character and permanence in Detroit's rapidly changing urban landscape.

Modern Amenities Image

Modern Living Integrated

Contemporary amenities seamlessly integrate with historic architecture. Premium fixtures and furnishings, Energy Star appliances, and in-unit laundry enhance both comfort and functionality. The thoughtful renovation honors the past while delivering all the conveniences expected in modern luxury residential living.

Community Connection Image

Community Cornerstone

Bagley Vision occupies a pivotal location at the intersection of Detroit's artistic heritage and its vibrant future. The building is a home, a gathering place, and a fixture of the community that respects its neighborhood roots while embracing new possibilities. Residents and commercial tenants become part of a living story, contributing to an evolving cultural landscape.
